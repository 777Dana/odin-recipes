Hashbrowns

Description

These are hashbrowns idk what to say about them but they are looking delicious.

Thanks to whoever created hashbrowns. I LOVE THEM!!!

Ingredients

  • 2 medium russet potatoes, shredded
  • 1/2 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup of oil for frying, or as needed
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Rinse shredded potatoes.
  2. Mix with onion, flour, and egg.
  3. Heat over medium high heat.
  4. Cook.
